Making Friends Through Cryptography, Open Source and Sharing

Focus Areas

Side-channel security analysis of cryptographic hardware

Development Goals

Improve the efficiency of hardware security analysis, reduce the cost of such analysis, and build an open-source platform for cryptographic hardware security analysis technology. This platform facilitates learning and communication among researchers and practitioners, enabling hardware security analysis enthusiasts at different technical levels to obtain corresponding theoretical and practical support, and jointly promoting rapid development in the field of cryptographic hardware security analysis.

Team History

Beijing Pairuiwei Technology Co., Ltd. was founded with investment from an innovation and entrepreneurship team led by Professor Wuliji of Tsinghua University, leveraging the deep expertise of the Information Security Laboratory of the School of Integrated Circuits at Tsinghua University in the field of cryptographic hardware side-channel analysis. The company brings together a team of top experts dedicated to providing comprehensive one-stop hardware security services, helping you easily tackle various security analysis challenges.
